Does it pay to be prepared?

Does it make sense to have cards on hand to be prepared for different occasions as they come up?

Depending on your lifestyle, it might make sense to have cards available especially if you have a very busy schedule. You can just reach into your box of cards and pull out what you need when you need it. It reduces the stress of knowing you have to go to the store and it saves you time.
